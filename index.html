Today

I'm so glad my daddy can play with me

When my daddy started to learn coding and web development, I couldn't have known what does it mean. He was away during the night and early morning to work hard, so he had to sleep until noon. After that, he would type on his computer and could barely have time to play with me.

I remember he was so happy when he got his first job as a web developer. He took me out for an ice cream and we played in the park for hours. He could finally stay at home and play with me and my little brother. I still don't know what is that that he does, but I love it, because he is always there for me.
